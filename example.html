3 Dev Adam

3 Dev Adam (English: 3 Giant Men) is a 1973 Turkish cult superhero film featuring Aytekin Akkaya as Captain America and Yavuz Selekman as Santo called to Istanbul on a special mission to stop the villainous Spider-Man and his criminal gang. The film, which went on nationwide general release across the country on November 1, 1973, was completely unauthorized by the copyright owners of the characters depicted.
